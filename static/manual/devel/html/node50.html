Initialize the second-order model


INTERFACE:

DESCRIPTION:

This subroutine computes the 's defined in (61) and the 's defined in (65) from the model parameters of the pressure redistribution models (53) and (59). Parameter sets from different authors are converted to the GOTM notation according to the relations discussed in section 4.5.
