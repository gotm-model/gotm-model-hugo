    Glossary of Terms#

    A#

    Absolute Salinity (SA): True mass fraction of dissolved material in seawater (g/kg). Accounts for spatial variations in composition. See TEOS-10.

    Albedo: Fraction of incident solar radiation reflected from a surface (dimensionless, 0-1).

    Adiabatic: Process occurring without heat exchange with surroundings.

    B#

    Barotropic: Depth-independent; related to external pressure gradients from sea surface slope.

    Baroclinic: Depth-dependent; related to internal pressure gradients from density variations.

    Buoyancy Frequency (N²): Measure of stratification strength; N² = -g/ρ₀ ∂ρ/∂z.

    C#

    Conservative Temperature (Θ): TEOS-10 temperature variable proportional to potential enthalpy. Better conserved under mixing than potential temperature.

    COARE: Coupled Ocean-Atmosphere Response Experiment; bulk flux algorithm.

    Coriolis Parameter (f): 2Ω sin(φ), where Ω is Earth’s rotation rate and φ is latitude.

    D#

    Dissipation Rate (ε): Rate of turbulent kinetic energy conversion to heat through viscous forces (m²/s³).

    Drag Coefficient (Cd): Dimensionless coefficient relating wind stress to wind speed squared.

    E#

    Eddy Diffusivity (κₜ): Turbulent diffusion coefficient for scalars (m²/s).

    Eddy Viscosity (νₜ): Turbulent viscosity for momentum (m²/s).

    EOS: Equation of State; relates density to temperature, salinity, and pressure.

    F#

    FABM: Framework for Aquatic Biogeochemical Models.

    Friction Velocity (u)*: Velocity scale for turbulence; u* = √(τ/ρ₀).

    G#

    GLS: Generic Length Scale; framework unifying k-ε, k-ω, and Mellor-Yamada models.

    H#

    Hydrostatic: Assumption that vertical pressure gradient is balanced by gravity; ∂p/∂z = -ρg.

    K#

    KPP: K-Profile Parameterization; boundary layer turbulence scheme.

    L#

    Latent Heat Flux: Energy exchange due to evaporation/condensation (W/m²).

    Longwave Radiation: Infrared thermal radiation (W/m²).

    M#

    Mixed Layer Depth (MLD): Depth of well-mixed surface layer.

    Mellor-Yamada: Family of turbulence closure models (MY2.5, MY2, etc.).

    P#

    Patankar Scheme: Numerical method ensuring positivity of production-destruction equations.

    Potential Temperature (θ): Temperature a water parcel would have if moved adiabatically to surface.

    Practical Salinity (SP): Dimensionless salinity based on conductivity ratio (PSS-78).

    R#

    RANS: Reynolds-Averaged Navier-Stokes equations.

    Reynolds Number (Re): Ratio of inertial to viscous forces.

    Richardson Number (Ri): Ratio of stratification to shear; Ri = N²/S².

    S#

    Sensible Heat Flux: Direct thermal energy exchange between air and water (W/m²).

    Shortwave Radiation: Solar radiation in visible and near-IR (W/m²).

    Sigma Coordinates: Terrain-following vertical coordinates.

    Stability Functions: Functions relating eddy viscosity/diffusivity to stratification and shear.

    T#

    TEOS-10: Thermodynamic Equation of Seawater - 2010; modern standard for seawater properties.

    TKE: Turbulent Kinetic Energy; k = ½⟨u’²+v’²+w’²⟩ (m²/s²).

    Z#

    Z-coordinates: Geopotential (fixed depth) vertical coordinates.
